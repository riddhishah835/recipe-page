Delicious Pancakes

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup whole wheat flour (or all-purpose flour)
  • ½ cup milk
  • 2–3 tbsp jaggery powder (for sweetness)
  • 1 tsp vanilla essence (optional)
  • ½ tsp baking powder
  • Pinch of cinnamon powder (optional, for flavor)
  • 1–2 tbsp butter (for cooking)

Instructions:

  1. Mix the dry ingredients> In a bowl, combine flour, baking powder, jaggery (or sugar), and cinnamon.
  2. Prepare the batter
    3. Slowly add milk and whisk to form a smooth batter (slightly thick, like regular pancake batter). Add vanilla essence if using.
  3. Cook the pancakes
    4. Heat a pan, spread a little ghee/butter. Pour a ladle of batter and cook on medium flame until bubbles appear. Flip and cook the other side until golden.
  4. Serve
  5. Stack them up, drizzle with honey, maple syrup, or melted chocolate.
  6. Top with nuts, raisins, or fresh fruits for extra sweetness.

    7. Enjoy your homemade pancakes
